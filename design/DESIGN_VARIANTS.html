What's the difference between transformers and RNNs?

Transformers process all tokens in parallel via self-attention. RNNs process sequentially — slower to train but lighter on memory for short sequences.

The attention mechanism allows transformers to capture long-range dependencies directly, which RNNs struggle with due to vanishing gradients.
